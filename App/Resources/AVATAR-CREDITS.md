# Avatar credit

## Current public companion

`AitvarasAvatar.glb` originates from
[`avatars/brunette.glb`](https://github.com/met4citizen/TalkingHead/blob/main/avatars/brunette.glb)
in Mika Suominen's MIT-licensed
[TalkingHead](https://github.com/met4citizen/TalkingHead) project. It is a
Ready Player Me-generated character with ARKit and Oculus viseme blendshapes.
Aitvaras uses those blendshapes for blinking, expressions and voice-driven lip
sync.

This human character is a temporary public fallback. The intended long-term
companion is an original small dragon based on the aitvaras of Lithuanian
folklore. A procedural dragon prototype exists in
`App/Companion/AvatarView.swift`, but it does not yet meet the project's final
visual-quality target.
